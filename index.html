Powerhouse of packed nutrients and yet stupendously delicious

High in Potassium

Potassium is good for your heart health and blood pressure. Its movement in and out of cells helps maintain a regular heartbeat.

High in Vitamin B6

Vit B6 helps production of haemoglobin. Haemoglobin is essential for blood circulatory system.

Good source of Magnesium

Magnesium helps to regulate Calcium and Vit D. Magnesium is vital for healthy bone formation in the body.
